Academy IT


As an RTO - Academy IT provides accredited training at Certificate &
Diploma Levels and operates under strict compliance and quality
assurance guidelines.

Academy IT are both a 'Microsoft Certified Partner - Learning' and a
Nationally Accredited 'Registered Training Organisation' (RTO:40358)

We provide Microsoft Official Training with certified instructors with
industry experience in the technologies they train.

We can support your personal and professional technical training
requirements.

Our Trainers


Academy IT utilises trainers that have industry and vocational training
qualifications and certifications.

Your trainer understands the content they deliver because they have the
background industry experience to know what it takes to employ
the technology successfully in the workplace, as well as the vendor
certifications required to demonstrate competence in the technology
required for training.

Academy IT also has trainer connections Australia wide to provide in-person
training in any location in Australia if a live-online solution is not an
option.
